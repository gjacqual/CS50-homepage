About Me

On this site I want to share my impressions about my travels and hikes. I will not start with a few stories. You will find a brief biography of the author in the "About" section . In the "My travels" section, I will gradually add more and more new stories.

I was born in 1986. Since childhood, I have loved traveling very much. From an early age, my parents took me hiking in the forests and mountains.

At the age of 10, I discovered mountains for the first time and fell in love with them.

At the age of 16 I became interested in downhill skiing, and at the age of 23 I also became interested in snowboarding.

Since that time, every year I try to go snowboarding in the mountains. The mountains are beautiful in winter and warm weather. In the summer, my friends and I go hiking in the forests, mountains.

Max Gjacqual - 2021
